Graph is a non-linear Data Structure

Nodes are represented by Circles

Edges are represented by Circles

Edges could be:

1. directed, undirected & bidirected
2. weighted & unweighted
3. connected & disconnected ( not-connected )
4. cyclic & acyclic
5. sparse & dense
